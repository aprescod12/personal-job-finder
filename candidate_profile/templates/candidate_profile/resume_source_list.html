THE FILE IS EVIDENCE, NOT THE PROFILE.

  1. StoreKeep the original document and immutable fingerprint.
  2. ReadConvert PDF, DOCX, or TXT content into local document text.
  3. ExtractA configured provider creates structured claims with evidence.
  4. ReviewEdit and approve or reject each claim individually.
  5. ApplyPersist approved claims with provenance; leave manual fields untouched.
